iSecorm Medical & Fertility Center


Services focus on helping individuals and couples who are facing challenges in conceiving naturally.

Fertility care involves diagnosing and treating underlying issues such as hormonal imbalances, ovulation disorders, or low sperm count. IVF is an advanced assisted reproductive technique where eggs and sperm are combined outside the body, and the resulting embryo is transferred to the uterus. These services may include ovulation stimulation, infertility testing, and IVF procedures.

Dr. Isaac Secorm

Dr. Isaac Secorm is a dedicated medical doctor with broad clinical experience and a specialty in reproductive health. He is the founder of iSecorm Medical & Fertility Center, where he provides expert fertility care to individuals and couples seeking to conceive naturally or through assisted methods. Dr. Secorm has practiced at both Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital and the 37 Military Hospital, gaining valuable experience across various medical disciplines. Though well-versed in general medicine, his passion lies in helping patients overcome fertility challenges using modern, patient-centered approaches. An alumnus of PRESEC-Legon, Dr. Secorm combines academic excellence with compassionate care to make quality reproductive services more accessible in Ghana.
