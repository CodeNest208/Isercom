OUR SERVICES

Consultation & Specialized Clinics

OPD consultations serve as the first point of contact, offering general medical care for common illnesses, preventive check-ups, and basic treatments. Specialized Clinic consultations are conducted by expert doctors for occupation-specific medical needs.

Antenatal Care

Medical and supportive care given to women throughout their pregnancy. This aims to monitor the health of both mother and the developing baby to ensure a safe pregnancy and delivery.

Obstetrics & Gynaecological Service

Obstetrics and Gynaecological (OB/GYN) services focus on women’s reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth. It includes diagnosis and treatment of conditions, and professional support for both men and women.

Fertility & IVF

IVF is an advanced assisted reproductive technique where eggs and sperm are collected outside the body, and the resulting embryo is transferred to the uterus.
