Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your personal health information.

Important: This privacy policy explains how Isercom Clinic collects, uses, and protects your personal health information. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Date of birth and demographic information
  • Insurance information and emergency contacts
  • Medical history and health conditions
  • Treatment records and test results
  • Payment and billing information

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our online services, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referral source and exit pages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal health information for the following purposes:

Treatment

  • Providing medical care and treatment
  • Coordinating care with other healthcare providers
  • Maintaining your medical records
  • Emergency medical situations

Payment

  • Processing payments and insurance claims
  • Billing for services provided
  • Verifying insurance coverage
  • Financial assistance programs

Healthcare Operations

  • Quality improvement activities
  • Staff training and education
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Customer service and support

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal health information. We only share your information as described in this policy or as required by law.

Authorized Disclosures

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Insurance companies for payment and coverage verification
  • Laboratory and imaging facilities for diagnostic services
  • Pharmacies for prescription fulfillment
  • Family members involved in your care (with your consent)

Required Disclosures

We may be required to disclose your information:

  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • For public health activities and disease prevention
  • To report suspected abuse or neglect
  • For law enforcement purposes as required by law
  • To prevent serious threats to health or safety

4. Your Rights

Under federal and state privacy laws, you have the right to:

Access Your Records

  • Request copies of your medical records
  • Review your health information
  • Obtain a list of disclosures we have made

Amend Your Records

  • Request corrections to your medical records
  • Add statements to your file
  • Update your contact information

Restrict Uses

  • Request restrictions on how we use your information
  • Ask us not to disclose information to certain parties
  • Opt out of certain communications

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal health information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security monitoring and updates
  • Access controls and user authentication

Administrative Safeguards

  • Staff training on privacy and security
  • Policies and procedures for data handling
  • Regular compliance audits
  • Incident response procedures

Physical Safeguards

  • Secured facilities and restricted access
  • Locked file cabinets and offices
  • Secure disposal of records
  • Workstation and device security

6. Website Privacy

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and features

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Notify you of significant changes
  • Update the "Last Modified" date
  • Provide notice as required by law
Notice: This policy is effective as of the date listed below and applies to all personal health information in our possession.
