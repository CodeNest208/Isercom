What services does Isercom Clinic provide?

Isercom Clinic offers comprehensive healthcare services including:

  • Primary care and general medicine
  • Fertility and reproductive health services
  • Women's health and gynecology
  • Specialized medical consultations
  • Diagnostic testing and imaging
  • Preventive care and health screenings
What are your office hours?

Our office hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Emergency consultations are available 24/7. Please call our emergency line for urgent medical needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We also offer:

  • Direct billing to insurance companies
  • Flexible payment plans
  • Self-pay options with discounted rates
  • Financial assistance programs for qualifying patients

Please contact our billing department to verify your specific insurance coverage.

üìÖ Appointments & Scheduling

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule appointments through several convenient methods:

  • Online: Use our website's appointment booking system (available 24/7)
  • Phone: Call us at (555) 123-4568 during business hours
  • In-person: Visit our front desk to schedule
  • Patient Portal: Log into your online account

For urgent appointments, please call our office directly.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice when canceling appointments. This helps us:

  • Offer your appointment time to other patients
  • Avoid no-show fees
  • Maintain efficient scheduling

A no-show fee may be charged for appointments missed without adequate notice. Repeated no-shows may affect your ability to schedule future appointments.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to:

  • Complete any necessary paperwork
  • Update your insurance information
  • Review your medical history
  • Handle any billing questions

For new patients, we recommend arriving 30 minutes early to complete registration forms.

What fertility treatments do you offer?

Our fertility specialists provide comprehensive reproductive health services:

  • Fertility testing and evaluation
  • In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  • Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)
  • Egg freezing and preservation
  • Male fertility testing and treatment
  • Fertility counseling and support

We create personalized treatment plans based on your unique needs and circumstances.

Do I need a referral for fertility services?

Referral requirements depend on your insurance plan:

  • Some insurance plans: Require referrals from primary care physicians
  • Self-pay patients: No referral needed
  • Direct-pay plans: Usually no referral required

We recommend checking with your insurance provider or contacting our office to verify requirements.

When is payment due?

Payment policies:

Contact our billing department to discuss payment plan options that work for your situation.
